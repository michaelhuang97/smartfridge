Shrimp Scampi

Ingredients:


  • 3 to 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup butter, cubed
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 pound uncooked medium shrimp
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
  • Angel hair pasta
  • 3 to 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup butter, cubed
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 pound uncooked medium shrimp
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
  • Angel hair pasta

Cooking Instructions:


  • 1
    In a 10-in. skillet, saute garlic in butter and oil until fragrant. Add the shrimp, lemon juice, pepper and oregano; stir until shrimp turn pink.

  • 2
    Broil 6 in. from the heat for 2-3 minutes or until topping is golden brown. Constantly stir as to prevent sticking.

  • 3
    Serve with pasta. Sprinkle remaining grated Parmesan cheese, bread crumbs, and parsley.
